Chr16 top100 HyenaDNA-small-32k modeling milestone
==================================================

Dataset:
- chr16 selected top100 regions
- up to 50 reads per region
- samples: merged_e5b and merged_c1
- input: DNA sequence only
- targets: CpG 5mC probabilities and adenine 6mA probabilities
- train/val/test split by genomic region

Main result:
- 5mC is consistently more predictable from sequence than 6mA.
- 6mA has weak but real sequence-associated signal.
- Combining C1 and E5B is stable.
- Adding sample_id gives small improvements in ranking/correlation and improves 6mA calibration, but does not solve the harder 6mA task.

Best current combined sample-conditioned test metrics:
- 5mC: Pearson 0.454, Spearman 0.351, AUROC 0.707, AUPRC 0.758
- 6mA: Pearson 0.193, Spearman 0.149, AUROC 0.672, AUPRC 0.112

Interpretation:
This is now a valid sequence-only + sample-context baseline for the thesis pipeline.
The next biological/modeling improvement should be scaling to more regions/chromosomes
or adding richer context, rather than further small architecture tuning on chr16 only.
