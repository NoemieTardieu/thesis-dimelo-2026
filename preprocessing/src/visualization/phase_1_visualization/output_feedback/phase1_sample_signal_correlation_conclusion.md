# Phase 1 Sample Comparability Conclusion

Pearson correlation was computed across common 1 kb genomic bins using local mC and mA signal summaries derived from the DiMeLo-seq data.

- Mean off-diagonal mC correlation: 0.889
- Mean off-diagonal mA correlation: 0.280

Across the three DiMeLo-seq samples, local mC levels were more consistent across samples than local mA levels. In contrast, the mA signal showed stronger mark-dependent divergence, consistent with the interpretation that the adenine-derived signal is more specific to the targeted chromatin context.
